Fundamental of Calculus

This course is the series of University Calculus for science and engineering. It covers Real number system, inequalities, functions and limits, derivatives, applications of derivatives, integrals, applications of integrals, transcendental functions, techniques of integration, indefinite forms, improper integrals, infinite series, 2D and 3D geometries, derivatives in Rn, double integrals, ordinary differential equations.

Basics of Calculus: Numbers and Functions

This session introduce students to basics of calculus, basic operations, and functions in university calculus. This session includes materials about:

  1. Types of Numbers
  2. Linear Equation with Two and Three Variables
  3. Inequalities and Absolute Value
  4. Domain and Range of Function
  5. Cartesian, Cylindrical, and Spherical Coordinate System
  6. Algebraic Function
  7. Trigonometric Function
  8. Composition of Several Function
